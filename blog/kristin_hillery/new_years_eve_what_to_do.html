New Year’s Eve: What to do?

31 December 2007 | Kristin Hillery

Yawn. It’s New Year’s Eve. You’re probably waiting to hear about the perfect party – the one where there’s no cover, an open bar, all your best friends will be there, and it’s right outside your door so you won’t have to worry about parking or getting home safely. Since that’s probably not going to happen, here are a few ideas on what to do tonight:

Second Sunday Sock Hop

So it’s definitely not the second Sunday of the month, but that’s not going to keep The Scoot Inn from putting on what might be their best-loved event: The Second Sunday Sock Hop. Dress in your finest vintage clothing – ’40s, ’50s, ’60s… it honestly doesn’t matter – and dance in the new year.

The Scoot Inn
10pm–2am | $10

Austin Steam Train: New Year’s Eve Twilight Flyer

Twilight Flyer passengers will hop onboard after walking down a red carpet – they’re going all out to make sure you get VIP treatment. Once onboard, expect music from the “Glamour Stars” of Laughing Out Loud Productions who will be singing as Elvis, Marilyn Monroe, Miss Understood Texas, and more. If this sounds kind of awkward, don’t worry – there’s an open bar with beer, wine, and champagne. There will also be a gourmet buffet. The train leaves from Cedar Park, and they recommend you reserve your tickets as soon as possible by calling 477-8468.

9pm–1am | $125 per person | 477-8468 to get tickets and more info

Ararat Rising

Food, drinks, tarot readings, drag kings, and fire dancing at this Mercury Hall party should make for an entertaining evening. There’s a pomegranate champagne toast at midnight that will be led by the party’s master of ceremonies, Woody B. Goode, and Trevor N. Deavor. DJ MaryMac and Dr Bullet will be spinning; a buffet from Ararat and organic coffee bar from Hot Mama’s will be provided. Though tickets are pricey at $50 a piece, you can BYOB.

Mercury Hall
8pm–2am | $50 per person | Get tickets

Joe Rogan at Cap City

You may know him from Fear Factor, or maybe you’ve seen him perform at Cap City before. Comedian Joe Rogan is in town to make your New Year’s Eve as funny as possible.

Cap City Comedy Club
8pm and 10:30pm | $30–$71.50 | Get tickets
