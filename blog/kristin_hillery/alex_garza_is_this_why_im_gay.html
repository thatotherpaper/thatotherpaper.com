Alex Garza: ‘Is This Why I’m Gay?’

15 May 2007 | Kristin Hillery
Alex Garza’s Is This Why I’m Gay? 

Local comedian and actor Alex Garza performs Is This Why I’m Gay? at 8pm tonight and tomorrow night at the Vortex. In its second night tonight, Alex’s one-man show borrows material from his stand-up acts and tries to define what makes a gay man gay.

From the promoter:

Through sometimes dark and sarcastic humor, Alex Garza questions the nature v. nurture debate and blames his parents for turning him to the queer side. He depicts the traumatic horrors of dating the female species, and the even more psychotic realities of dating men. He confesses his life-long dream of becoming a Grammy-award winning gay rapper. General Peter Pace, George Michael, The Facts of Life, Matthew McConaughey, and even Sweet Pea are all part of Alex Garza’s quest to discover the true beginnings of his so-called pink triangle loving, rainbow riding, queer life.

8pm May 14–16 | Pick your price: $10–$30

The Vortex
2307 Manor Road • 478-5282
