Dollar ‘Office’ supplies at your local Target

16 September 2007 | Kristin Hillery
photo / Kristin Hillery 

Target’s $1 section – awesomely located near the entrance of most Target stores – just got a billion times better. I’m not sure how long they’ve had this stuff, but right now you can find notepads, magnets, pens, pencils, stress balls, and mugs from The Office there for a buck each.

I found the stuff pictured above this morning at the Target at 183 and Ohlen Rd.

The Office season premiere is 9pm Thursday, September 27 on NBC. OhgodIcantwait.
