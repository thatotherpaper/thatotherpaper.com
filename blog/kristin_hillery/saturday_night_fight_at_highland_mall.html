Saturday night fight at Highland Mall

10 April 2007 | Kristin Hillery

Highland Mall, what’s the deal?

So apparently things got a little crazy last Saturday night at Highland Mall. After a bunch of fights broke out – one of them found its way to YouTube, below – the mall was shut down by the APD and mall security at about 7pm.

KEYE quoted Nelson Linder of the NAACP, who said that “people were just tense about the crowds of African Americans in town for the Texas Relays, and all the black shoppers.”

Awk-waaaard!

But seriously, check out the video. It’s pretty clear that there were way too many people in the mall. Or were there?
