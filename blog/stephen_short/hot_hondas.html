Hot Hondas

29 October 2007 | Stephen Short
photo / Jemal 

Watch out Accord owners! The Austin Police Department has released vehicle theft statistics indicating that 358 of the popular Honda sedans have been broken into or stolen since January 2007. This is a marked increase of only 170 Honda Accord thefts in 2006.

Theft prevention tips from APD:

Lock your car.

Take your key.

Never leave your car running unattended, even for a minute.

Don’t hide spare keys on your vehicle. If you can find them, so can a thief.

Roll your windows up all the way, even in hot weather.

Park in well-lighted areas.

Park as near to an open business as possible.

Don’t leave valuables in open sight; lock them in the trunk.

If you have a garage, use it, and lock the garage door.
