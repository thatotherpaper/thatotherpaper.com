TOP picks: April 9 - 15

9 April 2007 | That Other Paper

Monday | April 9

Funniest Person in Austin contest

It’s that time of year again – this is week two of the Funniest Person in Austin contest. Seventeen comics are scheduled to compete tonight for a spot in the semi-finals May 14th and 15th. John Ramsey, winner of 2005’s FPIA contest, will host the show.
