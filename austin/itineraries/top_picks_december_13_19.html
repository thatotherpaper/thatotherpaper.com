TOP picks: Dec 13-19

13 December 2006 | That Other Paper

Wednesday | December 13

photo / Gallery Lombardi 

Xmas Expo

Over 50 artists’ work will be on display at Gallery Lombardi’s Xmas Expo, and pieces purchased may be taken home immediately to put under the tree. But even if you don’t need any last-minute gifts, come by to see some fabulous art in a gallery that won’t be around much longer – next month, Lombardi will close until the spring so that a new building can be constructed on the property.

Noon-6pm. Free.

512-Go!» Gallery Lombardi
910 West 3rd St • 481-1088

Thursday | December 14

TNA, yo 

Thursday Night Awesome

Yep. Thursday Night Awesome is back in this week’s TOP Picks. But we have very good reasons: Not only is this show as awesome as its title implies, but That Other Paper editor Eric Seufert will be doing a stand-up act tonight. Annnnd That Other Paper writer Chris Trew happens to be the host of the show. Improv troupes Kazillionaire, Wittle, and The Frank Mills will also perform.

Thursday Night Awesome
8pm. $7, first row sits free.

512-Go!» The Hideout
617 Congress Ave • Austin, TX 78701

Saturday | December 16

The Invisible Czars 

The Invisible Czars

It’s “The Nutcracker Suite” like you’ve never heard: The Invisible Czars reinvent Tchaikovsky’s classic by bringing in drums, guitars, bass, a keyboard, a trumpet, and a guest violinist.

The Invisible Czars
Kids’ show at 3pm, $5. Loud show at 7pm, $8.

512-Go!» Ruta Maya

Sunday | December 17

Blue Genie Art Bazaar 

Blue Genie Art Bazaar

Forget the mall – hit up Blue Genie Art Bazaar to find arty gifts like paintings, photos, posters, ceramics, jewelry, and sculptures. With over 80 artists participating in the bazaar, you’re bound to find a better present for your sister than a pug calendar.

Update Check out our Blue Genie Art Bazaar slideshow to sample the wares.

Blue Genie Art Bazaar
10am-10pm. Free.

Blue Genie Art Industries
916 Springdale Rd Bldg #4 • 444-6655

Monday | December 18

37th Street Lights

The folks on 37th Street go all out when it comes time to decorate their houses and yards with Christmas lights. This tradition has gotten so popular – by word of mouth alone – since it began in 1980 that you can’t drive down the street after sunset. But why wouldn’t you want to walk around and take your time to enjoy these amazing, creative displays?

The lights are located on 37th Street, just east of Guadalupe. Your best bet is to park somewhere a block or two away and walk over – seriously, don’t try to drive down 37th.

Check out our 37th Street Lights slideshow

Tuesday | December 19

100 Proof Comedy Tour 

100 Proof Comedy

Local comics Matt Bearden, Doug Mellard, Matt Sadler, and JR Brow make up 100 Proof Comedy. There’s really not much to say here: These talented guys are more than you could ever ask for in a stand-up show. Show’s hosted by Chris Allen.

100 Proof Comedy
8pm. Call 481-1088 for ticket info.

512-Go!» Cap City Comedy Club
910 West 3rd St • 481-1088
