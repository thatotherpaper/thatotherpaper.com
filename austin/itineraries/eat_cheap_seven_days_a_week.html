Eat cheap seven days a week

16 December 2006 | Kristin Hillery

Hey, you. Yeah, you – Mr. Thrifty. Grab a friend (or an even number of pals) and eat cheap all week with these fabulous two-for-one deals and bargain meals in the ATX.

Monday

Mondays are cheap at the original Alamo 

Two-for-one fajitas at Santa Rita Tex-Mex Cantina

After 3pm, you and a friend each get a platter of fajitas for a mere $8.99.

512-Go!» Santa Rita Cantina
1206 West 38th Street (in the 26 Doors shopping center) • 419-7482

Music Mondays at Alamo Drafthouse (downtown)

If you’d like a side of entertainment to go with your meal, check out a music-related film every Monday at 9:45pm for $2 (or $1 for students/seniors/AFS members). Food and drink specials: You’ll get a free large popcorn with the purchase of a bucket of beer, and a free large soda or #2 Alamo Ale when you buy a pizza.

512-Go!» Alamo Drafthouse downtown
409 Colorado St • 476-1320

Tuesday

Buy one 10-inch pepperoni pizza, get another one free at Frank & Angie’s

From 6pm to 10pm, you’ll get two pepperoni pizzas for the price of one.

512-Go!» Frank & Angie’s
508 West Ave • 472-3534

Chicken Fried Tuesdays at Threadgill’s

photo / Truman 

Two-for-one chicken fried chicken or chicken fried steak when you and a pal show your student ID cards. Note that this deal is only offered at the North Lamar location.

512-Go!» Threadgill’s
6416 North Lamar • 451-5440

Wednesday

photo / Sam Felder Kneel before Hut’s Hamburgers 

Hamburger Happy Hour at Hut’s

Enjoy two-for-one burgers at Hut’s from 6pm to 10pm.

512-Go!» Hut’s Hamburgers
807 W 6th St • 472-0693

Thursday

Quick dinner for $2.50 at Sandy’s Hamburgers

Sure, Sandy’s has some fantastic custard, but you might as well grab some non-frozen treats if you’re going to stand in that line for a thousand years. $2.50 will get you a cheeseburger, fries, and a soda.

512-Go!» Sandy’s Hamburgers
603 Barton Springs Rd • 478-6322

Friday

Half-price appetizers at Z’Tejas

Z’Tejas has half-price appetizers from 5pm to 7pm Monday through Friday, but you might as well save this one for Friday – it rules. Any appetizer on the menu, including catfish beignets, grilled jumbo quesadillas (with chicken, pork, or veggies), and pork and black bean nachos, is under five bucks. The plates are big enough to split between two or three people, too, so order a few and share ‘em. No drink specials, but the margaritas are very strong.

512-Go!» Z’Tejas
1110 West 6th Street • 478-5355

Saturday

$2.50 madness at Sandy’s Hamburgers

Sandy’s $2.50 cheeseburger/fries/soda deal is so good, why wouldn’t you do it twice in one week? Tap that.

Sunday

photo / Roland Tanglao 

Two-for-One spaghetti with marinara at Frank & Angie’s

From 6pm to 10pm on Sundays, stop by Frank & Angie’s to take advantage of their two-for-one spaghetti with marinara deal.

512-Go!» Frank & Angie’s
508 West Ave • 472-3534
