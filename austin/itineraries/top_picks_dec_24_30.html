TOP picks: Dec 24 - 30

23 December 2007 | That Other Paper

Monday | December 24

Christmas Eve

photo / willsfca Daddy? 

It’s the day before Christmas, and you’re probably out buying more gift cards for people you barely know, or you just want to spend the day relaxing in your pajamas. But if you’re looking to get out there and do something fun, here are a few options:

  • Ice skating at Whole Foods (6th and Lamar location)
  • Rock and Roll Karaoke at Beerland
  • A movie at your local theater
  • Rent a movie from your local video store
  • Santa vs the Snowman 3D at the Bob Bullock Texas State History Museum
  • Local Christmas lights displays

Tuesday | December 25

Christmas

It’s Christmas day, and everything is closed on the day you most need to get out of your house. What’s a person to do?

  • Go buy a book or just pretend you’re going to buy one and read a book you already own at BookPeople
  • Rent a movie. Most Blockbusters are open on Christmas Day.
  • The Alamo Drafthouse is open. Double whammy – Christmas dinner and a movie.
  • Try getting a latte at your local coffee house. If it’s closed, try a Starbucks on a major highway like I-35. Or Austin’s own 24-hour location at 2900 W Anderson Ln.
  • Go to the park.
  • Take a romantic stroll on Mount Bonnell.

And if all of that fails, drink eggnog with your family, play board games, and roast chestnuts by the fire.

Wednesday | December 26

Friday with no profanity

As part of the Alamo Drafthouse’s High for the Holidays series, staff members dubbed over the profanity in Friday using an upbeat, jovial, white middle-class man’s voice to insert euphemisms for common curse words or derogatory phrases. Hilarity shall ensue.

9:45pm | $8.25

Alamo Downtown
320 E 6th • Austin, TX 78701

Other options:

  • Flyjack at TC’s Lounge | 8pm
  • DJ Pop Noir at Lovejoy’s | 9pm

Thursday | December 27

The Thulsa Doom

Former Fiction members Luther Smalls and Matt Georgia also play in this band, which promises a “unique jam of electro-acoustic darkness.” The Whisky Bar is free, and they’ll be playing with The Snake Trap, Butcher Bear, and DJ Kon Karne. So pull on your tightest jeans, wear a headband, and keep an eye out for members of Spoon.

10pm | FREE

Whisky Bar
303 W 5th St • Austin, TX 78701

Other options:

  • Arc Angels at Antone’s | 8pm
  • Jesse Dayton at Broken Spoke | 7pm

Friday | December 28

photo / Kristin Hillery Well isn’t this convenient 

Matt Bearden

Matt Bearden, one of our favorite comedians, is featuring tonight at Cap City. He’s the winner of the Funniest Person in Austin contest (2002) and has been on Premium Blend and Austin Stories. Matt tours the country doing stand-up, and we should be proud that he calls Austin home. He’s quick, witty, brilliant, and never boring – this guy knows how to entertain an audience.

8, 10:30pm | $14–$17

Cap City Comedy Club
8120 Research Blvd • Austin, TX 78758

Other options:

  • JR Brow at The Velveeta Room | 9:30 and 11:30pm
  • Maneja Beto at Flamingo Cantina | Doors at 9pm

Saturday | December 29

UT Basketball: Texas Longhorns vs. Wisconsin Badgers

Tonight our Texas Longhorns play the Wisconsin Badgers. The Badgers have traveled all the way from Wisconsin for this ass whipping – and we won’t disappoint them. So come cheer the Longhorns on as they take it to the hole over and over again.

11am | $8–$35

Frank Erwin Center
1701 Red River • Austin, TX 78701

Other options:

  • Old 97s at La Zona Rosa | 8pm
  • Goldilox adn Neptune at The Parish Room | 9pm

Sunday | December 30

Same Year’s Eve

The Austin improv comedy community is gathering for an all-out-night-before-New-Year’s-Eve – Same Year’s Eve – extravaganza. With a smattering of forms of improv and a free party afterward, this is a great way to have some laughs or learn about Austin improv comedy. Guaranteed to be a good time.

8pm | $10

Hideout Theatre
617 Congress Ave. • Austin, TX 78701

Other options:

  • The Polar Express at the IMAX at Bob Bullock Texas State History Museum | 7pm
  • Take Me to Bed or Lose Me Forever at Volitant Gallery | 6pm
