Milk isn’t boring anymore

27 March 2008 | Christine Acker
courtesy Sippahh® Sippahh® Straws add flavor to my life 

Do you ever look down in your plain white milk in disgust and wish it wasn’t so darn boring? Sipahh® has solved this ages-old dilemma with its brilliant product, Milk Flavoring Straws. They’re basically straws with flavor bubbles inside them that you sip your milk through. There are four delicious flavors: chocolate, strawberry, banana and cookies & cream. They taste so good that you go “Ahhhh!” (Get it? Sip-ahh?)

I know these are supposed to be for kids, but I want them too. Since when do kids have the monopoly on un-boring milk?
