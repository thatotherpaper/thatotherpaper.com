I’ve got a really good feeling about our date tonight

2 January 2007 | John Barnet
photo / Gabu-chan 

Hey, you. Oh, sorry – just throw those Coke cans in the back seat. Yeah, those wrappers can go, too. Actually, I think there are a few M&Ms left in that one. Stick the bag in the glove compartment. Unless you want some.

I want to clear the air before we leave for the restaurant. I’m sure you’ve noticed by now – this is a pretty nice pick-up truck. But don’t think I’m some rich, arrogant jerk or anything. It’s a lease. I put like two grand down on it and I’m making payments. Three hundo a month. You like that CD player? Six disc.

And look, I want to make sure that you don’t have any expectations about tonight. Nothing’s going to happen. We’ll grab dinner, have a nice time, and maybe make out in the bed of my truck behind that big vasectomy reversal billboard down the road – but that’s it. I like you. We should take things slowly.

I’ve got a really good feeling about our date tonight.

I hate to jinx it, but I think this could be something serious. You know, like we vacation together. At a lake house or something. I only say that because I remember you telling me you have a lake house. I’m not doing anything next weekend. Just FYI.

You don’t have to say yes right now. It’s our first date – I want things to progress naturally. Do you like this song? It’s my brother’s band, The Nightmare Philharmonic. Do you have a favorite Shriek-core band? Thrash-metal? Spasm-rock? Well, what do you like? Country? Sorry, chica, but the first rule of the Black Maria is that the driver picks the tunes. Don’t worry, though – I had a few beers before picking you up tonight, so if I drink at all at the restaurant you’ll probably get to drive home.

Oh, I should have told you. I call my truck the Black Maria.

I hate to jump the gun here, but – well, look at this. I drew a tattoo design for us. I think we should each get one on our right shoulder. See, the skull’s mohawk is made out of fire, and his earring is barbed wire. You can’t really tell because it’s only one frame, but he’s screaming “radical.”

Ok, what the hell is this guy doing? What a jackass. He thinks he’s so cool with his pimp cane and oversized sunglasses. Sure, just cross the street whenever you want, buddy. I’m in no hurry; it’s not like my girlfriend and I are on the way to dinner or anything. Ha, good one – yeah, it is like he’s blind for trying cross when I’ve got the green light.

Oh. Yeah, I knew that. But still. Pretty inconsiderate.

Do you like that bobble head? I got it at Chuck E. Cheese’s last week. I didn’t win it – I actually exchanged my tickets for a toy squirt gun. I traded some kid for it, straight up. Can you believe that? A bobble head for a squirt gun! What an idiot. I knew those business classes I took at community college would pay off.

So how do you feel about kids? Just wondering. I don’t want any kids – you’ve got to be a moron to have kids. All they do is suck up your money and time. How am I supposed to save up for a new guitar if I’ve got to dump all of my hard-earned dough into some useless rugrat? Sounds like a raw deal to me. No, thanks – I’m no sucker.

Your sister just had a son? Wow, that’s amazing. I am so happy for her. This is really incredible. No, it’s just that I’ve never been an uncle before. Well, yeah, not technically, but, you know. By the time he can speak that’s what he’ll call me. Actually, that’s stupid. I’m going to be the cool uncle. He’ll call me Roy.

photo / Rich Anderson 

I never realized how badass this song is. Grab the wheel – I’ve got to air guitar this riff. Damn, that was sick! Did you see me finger-picking those notes? I was completely taken over by the jam. I’m just a passionate guy. I get excited about something and I have to take it to the extreme. You know what? Screw dinner. Here, take a tic-tac – we’re going to the vasectomy reversal billboard. We can stop by the corner store and grab some Boone’s Farm first. Hey, a guy can be passionate and still think ahead. Plus, I’m losing my buzz.

You’re feeling sick? Crap – it’s probably the air-conditioning. I think something died in one of the ducts. Well, is it cool if I drop you off at a bus stop, then? I can still make my softball game if I haul ass. I’d normally do the classy thing and take you home, but this is a league match. You can take some change from that cup holder for the bus.

Okay, that’s enough. Seriously, it doesn’t cost that much. Put a few of those quarters back.

Where do we stand on the lake house next week? Sure, I understand – have to run it by the folks. Well, give me a call. Or maybe I can stop by your place tonight after the game? Yeah, you’re right. I’ll just wait for you to call. I gave you a special ring tone: Seal’s “Kiss From a Rose.” I don’t like Seal, but Batman Forever is my favorite movie. Maybe I’ll bring it with me if I come over tonight. You know, if you call.
