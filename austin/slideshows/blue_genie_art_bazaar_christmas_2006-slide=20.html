Blue Genie Art Bazaar: Christmas 2006

17 December 2006 | Todd Ross Nienkerk

The Blue Genie Art Bazaar isn’t merely an art show – it’s a gallery, crafts show, and gift shop all rolled into one. Pick up truly unique, locally produced gifts for anyone on your list. Be warned, however: Art ain’t cheap. Those on a budget should stick to trinkets and stocking-stuffers.

From the Blue Genie Art Industries website:

Blue Genie Art specializes in custom artwork for home and business, as well as museums and government institutions. Our award winning design and creation sets us apart from other commercial art enterprises.
