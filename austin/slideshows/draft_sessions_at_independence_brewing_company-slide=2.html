Draft Sessions at Independence Brewing Company

24 January 2007 | Veronica Hansen
Previous | Image 2 of 15 | Next
Draft Sessions at Independence Brewing Company

That Other Paper stopped by Independence Brewing Company last Saturday night to check out Draft Sessions, a new monthly concert series that combines local bands and local brew. Foot Foot, Clap! Clap!, and DJs Orion and Jazz One provided the music; Independence provided the beer. (We highly recommend the Alamo Ale.)
