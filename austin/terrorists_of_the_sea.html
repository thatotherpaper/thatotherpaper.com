Terrorists of the sea

21 November 2007 | Dr. Dan Jerbrowski
photo/ sanbeiji Land invaders 

As if your ability to lower your standards while trying to get laid at 1:52am isn’t already scary enough, now there’s Qua, a club on 4th Street that has sharks under the dance floor. After all, no town is complete without an age-exclusive dance club where potentially man-eating sharks swim around under the floor, right?

Personally, the last thing I need is a club that brings all the horrors of sea life right into downtown Austin, but that’s life I guess.

I don’t go in the ocean. When I tell people that, they look at me like I just pissed on their dog or something. My buddy wanted me to go down to Galveston with him, but I refused. He pointed out to me that even little girls play in the surf. I believe his exact words were, “Stop being a pussy.”

But who’s really being unreasonable here?

Think of it like this: For starters, there are things that eat the things that will try to eat you in the ocean. And translucent glowing shit that will sting you, and in general all kinds of different predators that specialize in swimming, unlike you or me. I mean, I can’t speak for everyone, but desperately flailing around while a shark bites off my foot is not my idea of an adequate exit strategy. And dancing above sharks like a dead chicken above the frying pan isn’t much better.

photo/ katemonkey This could be your hand 

Do you think I’m joking? Okay, we’ll see who’s laughing when you’re strapping on that prosthetic limb for the next 50 years (or less than that if your dumb ass keeps getting back in the ocean). How am I supposed to go surfing, let alone dancing, if I’m worried about losing a limb to some shark that obviously doesn’t realize that, according to the FDA, human meat is not fit even for human consumption?

Along with a few screwdrivers, I’ve got about 40 types of cow hormones pulsing through my veins at any moment, not to mention some kind of weird saline solution preservative, because I like my meat pre-packaged and cut (Wal-Mart style, baby). Do you know how nasty that must taste the second time around (however delicious it is the first)? That’s why sharks always spit out the foot and move on to less toxic creatures – humans are nasty beyond comprehension. Now those sharks can get a good, solid look at our nastiness while we freak “safely” out of reach.

Fact: There were 96 shark attacks in 2006 (62 were “unprovoked,” whatever the fuck that means). Fact: I’m not very lucky and probably look delicious from below in spite of my rottenness. Fact: Only shallow people live by the beach. The evidence against the ocean is overwhelming.

So I don’t think I’m alone here in proposing the full and utter extinction of all sharks. Let’s start with the land invaders hanging out up there at Qua. Hey, sharks, let’s see you bite off my foot with a gaff lodged 16 inches deep in your face. That’s 3,000 years of human ingenuity you feel poking you in the brain.

Do we really need animals around that are so aggressive they can only mate after gorging themselves on various types of carcasses (otherwise they’ll try to eat each other)? Even sharks don’t like other sharks – how can they expect me to feel any different?

photo/ admiralty This could be your house 

Now, I know what you’re thinking: “Come on, Dan, be reasonable. You’re a doctor. You can’t just go sailing the high seas or busting into nightclubs committing sharkocide. It’s just not proper.” Well, let me tell you something, shark-lover: Yes I can. There’s a reason great white sharks are endangered: They fucking eat people.

And I’m a person. People invented computers and HBO and lots of other awesome things. But what the hell have sharks ever contributed? Not much, I bet. And watch out snakes, you might be next. You’d better quit slithering around all scary, trying to pump poison into everyone – if you want to live.

Which brings me to my point (I actually have a point this time). I’ve started a coalition to look into this whole shark controversy. It’s called Coalition for the Extinction of All Sharks and other Enemies, or C.E.A.S.E. But don’t let the acronym fool you; we fully intend to eradicate all sharks and other enemies in the sea and on land. There will be no ceasing or desisting. That much I promise.

I know there are going to be some people who don’t agree with what I’m saying here. Unfortunately I can’t go around tasering and hog-tying all of them. At least not yet. But look at it this way. Sharks are un-American. We don’t generally put up with terrorism on land, and I don’t think we should put up with it in the sea. Don’t be a communist. God hates communists. He really only respects those who are willing to kill for what they believe in. Plus, I heard that sharks cause cancer. You’re either for us or against us. Shark sympathy is treason.
